# ETSY ADS PLAYBOOK (New Shop)

## Rule 1
Do not advertise everything. Ads only for proven organic listings.

## Trigger to start ads
- Listing has organic impressions and at least one favorite/click signal.
- Listing economics show >=30% margin before ads.

## Budget tiers
- Tier A (testing): $5/day total, 3-5 listings
- Tier B (validated): $15/day total, top 10 listings
- Tier C (scale): $30/day total, only high ROAS listings

## Stop-loss
Pause any listing after spend reaches $8 without conversion.

## Review cadence
- Daily: spend, clicks, CPC
- Every 72h: ROAS, conversion, profit after ads

## Goal metrics
- CTR > cohort median
- Conversion > cohort median
- Positive net profit after ad spend
