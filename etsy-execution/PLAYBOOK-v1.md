# PLAYBOOK v1 - Etsy POD Experiment Engine

## Mission
Run fast, data-driven listing experiments and compound winners.

## Daily cadence
1. Publish 12-20 listings from listing-backlog-1000.csv
2. Optimize 20 existing listings (title image price tags)
3. Update ops-dashboard-template.csv
4. 72h decision loop on cohorts

## 72h rules
- Kill: low impressions + low CTR after one rewrite
- Iterate: good impressions but no orders
- Scale: orders + >=30% estimated margin

## Scale actions
- Clone winner text into 5 variants
- Expand to 2 more products
- Add personalization variant (Name/Year/Role)

## Weekly review
- Top 10 winners by net profit
- Top 10 losers with failure reason
- Next week publish mix: 60% proven themes, 40% new bets

## Safety/IP
- Avoid trademarks, brand names, copyrighted characters
- Keep all copy generic/original
